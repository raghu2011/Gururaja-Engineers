About Gururaja Engineers

With over 25 years of expertise in parboil machinery manufacturing

Pioneers in Parboil Technology

Established in 1998, Gururaja Engineers has been at the forefront of designing and manufacturing advanced parboil machinery for the rice processing industry. Our commitment to quality and innovation has made us a trusted partner for rice mills across India and internationally.

We specialize in custom-engineered solutions that optimize the parboiling process, enhancing rice quality while reducing energy consumption and operational costs.
